About RIED

Building the bridge from research to revenue.

Structured pathways between Research, Innovation and Revenue — for founders, researchers and institutions alike.

Who We Are

Translating ideas into enterprises

RIED (Research Innovation Entrepreneurship Development) Pvt. Ltd. is building a structured ecosystem focused on translating research, innovation and ideas into scalable revenue generating outcomes.

Through the AI Powered RIED Engine and ecosystem activation programs, RIED connects academia, startups, funding pathways and commercialization systems.

Ecosystem Reach

Rooted in research, built for founders

Our work starts where academic research often stalls — at the edge of the lab — and carries it through validation, funding readiness and market entry alongside the founders and institutions building it.

With operations spanning Bengaluru, Pune and rural Bihar, RIED is designed to reach researchers and entrepreneurs wherever the next enterprise-ready idea is forming.

Our Mission

To build a scalable and AI-enabled research-to-revenue infrastructure that transforms ideas and innovation into measurable economic outcomes.

About RIED

Building structured pathways between Research, Innovation and Revenue.

Who We Are

RIED (Research Innovation Entrepreneurship Development) Pvt. Ltd. is building a structured ecosystem focused on translating research, innovation and ideas into scalable revenue generating outcomes.

Through the AI Powered RIED Engine and ecosystem activation programs, RIED connects academia, startups, funding pathways and commercialization systems.

Our Mission

To build a scalable and AI-enabled research-to-revenue infrastructure that transforms ideas and innovation into measurable economic outcomes.

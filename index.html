Frequently Asked Questions

RIED works with researchers, founders, startups, universities and incubators who want structured support turning research, ideas or innovation into a revenue-generating venture — from very early idea stage through to scale-up.

RIVS (RIED Idea Validation Score) and RRCI (RIED Research Commercialization Index) are RIED's proprietary evaluation frameworks. RIVS scores an idea's viability, scalability, execution readiness and ecosystem alignment; RRCI assesses a research project's market potential, commercialization feasibility and industry relevance.

What is RIED

RIED is a mission-oriented framework focused on translating academic research, patents, prototypes, and grassroots innovations into scalable enterprises and socio-economic outcomes. By bridging the gap between ideas and implementation, RIED helps unlock India's vast potential in science, technology, and entrepreneurship.

RIED operates at the intersection of academia, innovation ecosystems, startups, rural and semi-urban institutions, industry, and public systems, ensuring that promising ideas move meaningfully from labs and papers to markets and people. Our holistic approach fosters collaboration and accelerates the commercialization process for long-term benefits.

Why RIED Exists

India produces vast intellectual capital and grassroots innovation. Yet a large proportion remains unrealized—never becoming companies, solutions, or livelihoods. This results in missed opportunities for economic growth, job creation, and societal advancement in both urban and rural areas.

The gap is not intent or intelligence. The gap lies in:

  • research translation,
  • entrepreneurship development,
  • market integration, and
  • scale readiness,

particularly within semi-urban and rural academic ecosystems. These regions often face resource constraints, limited networks, and challenges in navigating complex markets, leading to innovative ideas stalling at early stages.

RIED exists to bridge this gap by building structured, outcome-driven pathways from Discovery → Enterprise → Development. We provide the tools, mentorship, and connections needed to transform potential into progress, creating a more inclusive innovation landscape across India.

What RIED Does

RIED enables institutions and innovators through a comprehensive suite of services tailored to India's unique ecosystem. We work closely with partners to identify, evaluate, and scale innovations, ensuring they deliver real-world value.

  • Research Translation & Patent Evaluation: Assessing academic outputs for commercial viability and guiding the patenting process.
  • Innovation-to-Enterprise Pathways: Converting prototypes into market-ready products through structured programs and expert support.
  • Entrepreneurship Development within Institutions: Building internal ecosystems for students and faculty to launch startups with training and resources.
  • Rural & Agrarian Innovation Commercialisation: Tailored assistance for grassroots innovations in agriculture, helping them reach wider markets and improve livelihoods.
  • Startup Scale-Up, Market Access & Funding Linkages: Connecting entrepreneurs to investors, markets, and growth opportunities for sustainable expansion.

The focus is always on outcomes—prototype to pilot, research to revenue, and ideas to impact. Through these efforts, RIED not only boosts economic development but also addresses societal challenges like rural empowerment and sustainable practices.

Our Impact

RIED is transforming India's innovation landscape by turning untapped potential into tangible results. Our efforts have led to significant socio-economic benefits, including job creation and rural development.

  • Translated over 150 academic patents and prototypes into viable enterprises, fostering economic growth.
  • Supported 200+ startups in rural and semi-urban areas, creating more than 1,000 jobs and empowering local communities.
  • Facilitated funding linkages worth ₹50 crore for innovators, enabling scale-up and market entry.
  • Partnered with 50+ institutions to build robust entrepreneurship ecosystems, enhancing innovation capacity.
  • Commercialized 75 agrarian innovations, boosting rural economies and sustainable practices.

Through these achievements, RIED continues to drive positive change, from improved agricultural practices to tech-driven solutions addressing everyday challenges in India.

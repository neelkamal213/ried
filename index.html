What is RIED

RIED is a mission-oriented framework focused on translating academic research, patents, prototypes, and grassroots innovations into scalable enterprises and socio-economic outcomes.

RIED operates at the intersection of academia, innovation ecosystems, startups, rural and semi-urban institutions, industry, and public systems, ensuring that promising ideas move meaningfully from labs and papers to markets and people.

Why RIED Exists

India produces vast intellectual capital and grassroots innovation. Yet a large proportion remains unrealized—never becoming companies, solutions, or livelihoods. This untapped potential represents missed opportunities for economic development, job creation, and societal progress.

The gap is not intent or intelligence. The gap lies in:

  • research translation,
  • entrepreneurship development,
  • market integration, and
  • scale readiness,

particularly within semi-urban and rural academic ecosystems. These areas often lack the resources and connections to commercialize ideas effectively.

RIED exists to bridge this gap by building structured, outcome-driven pathways from:

Discovery → Enterprise → Development

What RIED Does

RIED enables institutions and innovators through:

  • Research Translation & Patent Evaluation: Assess and refine academic outputs for commercial viability.
  • Innovation-to-Enterprise Pathways: Structured programs to convert prototypes into market-ready products.
  • Entrepreneurship Development within Institutions: Building internal ecosystems for student and faculty startups.
  • Rural & Agrarian Innovation Commercialisation: Tailored support for grassroots innovations in agriculture and rural sectors.
  • Startup Scale-Up, Market Access & Funding Linkages: Connecting entrepreneurs to investors, markets, and growth resources.

The focus is always on outcomes—prototype to pilot, research to revenue, and ideas to impact.

Our Core Areas

  • Translated over 150 academic patents and prototypes into viable enterprises.
  • Supported 200+ startups in rural and semi-urban areas, creating 1,000+ jobs.
  • Facilitated funding linkages worth ₹50 crore for innovators.
  • Partnered with 50+ institutions to build entrepreneurship ecosystems.
  • Commercialized 75 agrarian innovations, boosting rural economies.

Our work has led to real socio-economic outcomes, from improved agricultural practices to tech-driven solutions for everyday challenges.

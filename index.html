What is RIED

RIED is a mission-oriented framework focused on translating academic research, patents, prototypes, and grassroots innovations into scalable enterprises and socio-economic outcomes. We operate at the intersection of academia, innovation ecosystems, startups, rural and semi-urban institutions, industry, and public systems, ensuring that promising ideas move meaningfully from labs and papers to markets and people.

Our work empowers innovators by providing the tools, guidance, and networks needed to turn intellectual capital into tangible impact. Whether it's a university patent gathering dust or a rural inventor's prototype, RIED bridges the divide to create jobs, solutions, and sustainable growth.

Why RIED Exists

India produces vast intellectual capital and grassroots innovation. Yet a large proportion remains unrealized—never becoming companies, solutions, or livelihoods. This untapped potential represents missed opportunities for economic development, job creation, and societal progress.

The gap is not intent or intelligence. The gap lies in:

  • Research translation,
  • Entrepreneurship development,
  • Market integration, and
  • Scale readiness,

particularly within semi-urban and rural academic ecosystems. These areas often lack the resources and connections to commercialize ideas effectively.

RIED exists to bridge this gap by building structured, outcome-driven pathways from: Discovery → Enterprise → Development. We transform potential into progress, one innovation at a time.

What RIED Does

RIED enables institutions and innovators through a comprehensive suite of services designed to accelerate the journey from idea to impact:

  • Research Translation & Patent Evaluation: We assess and refine academic outputs for commercial viability.
  • Innovation-to-Enterprise Pathways: Structured programs to convert prototypes into market-ready products.
  • Entrepreneurship Development within Institutions: Building internal ecosystems for student and faculty startups.
  • Rural & Agrarian Innovation Commercialisation: Tailored support for grassroots innovations in agriculture and rural sectors.
  • Startup Scale-Up, Market Access & Funding Linkages: Connecting entrepreneurs to investors, markets, and growth resources.

The focus is always on outcomes—prototype to pilot, research to revenue, and ideas to impact. Our approach ensures that innovations don't just stay on paper but create real-world value.

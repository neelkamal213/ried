What is RIED

RIED is a mission-oriented framework focused on translating academic research, patents, prototypes, and grassroots innovations into scalable enterprises and socio-economic outcomes. RIED operates at the intersection of academia, innovation ecosystems, startups, rural and semi-urban institutions, industry, and public systems, ensuring that promising ideas move meaningfully from labs and papers to markets and people.
